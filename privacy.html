Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

WasteMatch ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our waste matching platform.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account information: name, email, phone number, business details
  • Waste load information: type, quantity, location, photos
  • Transaction data: matches, payments, communications
  • Usage data: platform interactions, preferences, device information

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Facilitate waste load matching between users
  • Process payments and transactions
  • Ensure EA compliance and generate waste transfer notes
  • Communicate with you about matches and platform updates
  • Improve our platform and services

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

We may share data only with:

  • Matched users (necessary for transactions)
  • Payment processors (Stripe) for transaction processing
  • Legal authorities if required by law or EA regulations

5. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@wastematch.co.uk

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure servers, and access controls.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations (including EA record-keeping requirements), and resolve disputes.

8. Contact Us

For privacy inquiries: privacy@wastematch.co.uk
