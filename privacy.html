Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

WasteMatch Ltd ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our waste matching platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Company details and site location
  • Material types and quantities
  • EA license numbers and verification documents

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Match you with verified buyers and sellers
  • Verify compliance with EA regulations
  • Process transactions and payments
  • Send you updates about matches and platform features
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Verified buyers/sellers for matching purposes
  • Payment processors for transaction handling
  • Regulatory bodies when required by law

5. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability

6. Contact Us

For privacy inquiries, contact: privacy@wastematch.co.uk
